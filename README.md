# leaderlens
LeaderLens is an AI-powered, automated analysis tool that processes SI session observation forms. It transforms forms into concise, actionable insights that surfaces program-wide trends along with strengths and weaknesses for specific SI Leaders.

## Peer Observations
### Metrics for Overall Peer Observations:
- How are all the openers?
- How are all the activities?
- How are all the closers? 
- What percentage of SILs are doing openers?
- How are most SILs interacting with their students?
- What could most people be improving on?
- What are most people doing well with?
- Most common red flags?

### Metrics for people observed:
- How was their opener?
- How were their activities?
- How was their closer?
- How did they interact with their students?
- What's one thing they could work on improving?
- What's one thing they did well?
- Any red flags?
